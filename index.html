Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

About Me

I am a passionate Data Analyst based in Málaga. I specialize in turning data into actionable insights and have a strong background in mathematics and data science. When I'm not analyzing data, I enjoy photography and cinema, going to the gym, and learning new things.

Experience

As a Data Analyst at Intrum, I specialize in the valuation and analysis of unsecured NPL portfolios across Europe using advanced tools such as R. I have developed and maintained Shiny applications to streamline the modeling and pricing prediction processes, as well as investment results reporting. My contributions have significantly enhanced and optimized the procedures for portfolio analysis and valuation by applying time series analysis techniques. Additionally, I have participated in more than 50 portfolio price evaluations in the various countries where the Swedish group operates.

Furthermore, I have worked as a freelance Data Analyst at Allianz, where I developed a customer satisfaction survey in five languages for twelve different services, sending out around 50,000 invitations each month. In this project, I improved the survey's aesthetics using basic knowledge of CSS, HTML, and JavaScript. I also created scripts to connect with the API of the tool to perform daily data extraction and cleaning.

Academic Background

My passion for data analysis led me to pursue a Master's degree in Big Data and Business Analytics at the Complutense University of Madrid, where I mastered cutting-edge techniques and tools in data analysis. Additionally, I deepened my theoretical knowledge through a Master's in Mathematical Engineering and Scientific Computing, specializing in stochastic differential equations and market studies.

Experiencia

Como analista de datos en Intrum, me dedico a la valoración y análisis de carteras NPL no garantizadas en Europa utilizando herramientas avanzadas como R. He desarrollado y mantenido aplicaciones Shiny para facilitar el proceso de modelización y predicción de precios, así como la elaboración de informes de resultados de inversiones. Mis contribuciones han mejorado y optimizado significativamente los procedimientos de análisis y valoración de carteras mediante la aplicación de técnicas de análisis de series temporales. Además, he participado en más de 50 evaluaciones de precio de portfolios en los distintos países en los que opera el grupo sueco.

Además, he trabajado como analista de datos freelance en Allianz, donde desarrollé una encuesta de satisfacción del cliente en cinco idiomas para doce servicios distintos, enviando alrededor de 50,000 invitaciones cada mes. En este proyecto, mejoré la estética de la encuesta utilizando conocimientos básicos de CSS, HTML y JavaScript. También creé scripts para conectar con la API de la herramienta y realizar la extracción y limpieza de datos diariamente.
